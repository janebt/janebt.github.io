Towards Christmas

Franciscan monastery of the Holy Spirit is a Bosnian Franciscan monastery in Fojnica, It was founded in 1668 and it includes a library of ca. 12,500 volumes, including 13 incunabula and 156 works written in Bosnian Cyrillic. It is a designated National Monument of Bosnia and Herzegovina. It is part of the Franciscan Province of Bosna Srebrena.
